What is mentoring?

This is the process of transferring knowledge and experience from the older generation to the younger one in the format of personal communication.

A mentor is a professional who is ready to share his unique knowledge and experience in the professional field you are interested in.

He will help you develop the necessary skills for your future career, help with scientific research and tell a couple of invaluable life stories.
